# ✈️ Airline Passenger Satisfaction & Flight Operations Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes airline passenger data to identify factors influencing customer satisfaction, flight delays, revenue generation, and operational efficiency. The dataset was intentionally designed with real-world data quality issues such as missing values, duplicate records, inconsistent formats, and outliers to simulate an industry-level data analysis workflow.

The objective of this project is to demonstrate the complete Data Analytics lifecycle, including data cleaning, preprocessing, exploratory data analysis (EDA), visualization, feature engineering, and business insight generation.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Airlines need to understand:

* What factors influence passenger satisfaction?
* How do flight delays affect customer experience?
* Which customer segments generate the highest revenue?
* Which routes are most frequently used?
* How can operational efficiency be improved?

## 🔍 Key Findings

### Customer Satisfaction

* Most passengers reported a satisfactory travel experience.
* Premium cabin passengers demonstrated higher satisfaction levels.

### Revenue Insights

* Business and First Class passengers generated the highest revenue.
* Returning customers contributed significantly to airline revenue.

### Operational Insights

* Departure delays strongly influenced arrival delays.
* A small number of flights experienced extreme delays.

### Customer Behavior

* Digital booking channels accounted for the majority of reservations.
* Certain routes consistently generated higher passenger traffic.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### 1. Improve Flight Punctuality

Reducing departure delays can significantly improve arrival performance and passenger satisfaction.

### 2. Enhance Economy Class Experience

Improving seat comfort and onboard services may increase customer satisfaction levels.

### 3. Strengthen Loyalty Programs

Targeting returning customers can improve retention and revenue growth.

### 4. Promote Premium Upgrades

Business and First Class passengers contribute a larger share of revenue and should be targeted through upgrade campaigns.

### 5. Expand Digital Booking Strategies

Website and mobile application bookings should remain a key focus area for growth.
